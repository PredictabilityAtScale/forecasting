# Throughput patterns: what changed this week?

Teams often miss turning weekly throughput data into clear decisions. This note gives a fast routine for every Friday.

## Weekly review checklist

- Pull the last 12 weeks of completed work items.
- Highlight weeks where capacity changed materially.
- Record one operational explanation for each outlier.
- Re-run the forecast with and without outliers.

## What to publish to stakeholders

1. Forecast range for the next target date.
2. Confidence level you are using.
3. A one-line risk statement.

> A short weekly rhythm beats a long monthly postmortem.
