# Slice work smaller to tighten forecast confidence

When story slices are inconsistent, your Monte Carlo outputs spread wider than needed.

## Three fast ways to improve slicing

- Set a lightweight definition of "small enough" for your team.
- Split blocked stories immediately, not at sprint boundaries.
- Track re-opened work and discuss root causes weekly.

## Watch these two signals

- **Right tail growth** in lead-time scatter plots.
- **Volatility spikes** after large epic handoffs.

Keep this weekly and your confidence bands usually narrow after 3–4 cycles.
